Description

There is a large variety of simulation games on the market at any given time; however, HARD TRUCK TYCOON by Valusoft and G5 Software for the PC gives players the opportunity to look at the inside workings of one of the global markets most valuable industries. Many people haven't ever really thought too much about those semi-trucks and their purpose except to get angry with them when they are driving on the Interstate. Despite the problems that big trucks may cause, they are the mainstay of the economy in almost every corner of the world. Without trucks, supply could never meet up with demand, and these are just a few factors that players will get to consider when working to build their company empire. One of the first things that players need to do is choose their vehicle for the fleet, then they will need to work on their company infrastructure and hire and train different drivers that they choose to work in their company. If players decide to choose a randomly generated mode of play, it will increase their ability to replay the game later. It will also allow them to face challenges and milestones that have existed in the shipping industry for the last forty to fifty years. HARD TRUCK TYCOON provides players who are looking for a slightly more difficult simulation game with a challenge every time they decide to enter the world they've designed for themselves.
